LiveNess probe
==============
Monitor the pod Service post pod created. If services in not running in pod or in hang ...New Pod will create and old pod delted.


apiVersion: apps/v1
kind: Deployment
metadata:
  labels:
    app: liveness1
  name: liveness1
spec:
  replicas: 3
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      app: liveness
  template:
    metadata:
      labels:
        app: liveness
    spec:
      containers:
      - image: devopstechlab1/liveness-nodejs-unhealthy
        name: web-liveness1
        livenessProbe:
          httpGet:
            path: /
            port: 8080
          initialDelaySeconds: 15
